Predicting Champions of European Soccer Leagues

Project Scope

We Choose to analyze the 5 largest European Leagues in soccer: La Liga, Premier League, Serie A, Bundesliga, and Ligue 1. We extracted data from footystats.org from the last 10 years. We hoped to that this data could help predict future league winners. After running multiple regression models and machine learning models with this data we found 30 of 190 inputs for each team every season were significant factors in determining the eventual league champion.
